# Healthcare-Patient-Visit-Analysis
This project analyzes hospital appointment data to understand patient behavior, no-show rates and common health conditions. The goal is to identify key trends and improve healthcare efficiency.
Author: Basanti Adhikari
The dataset includes information on:Patient gender and age, Appointment attendance (show/no-show), Medical conditions (hypertension, diabetes, alcoholism, disability)and Appointment dates and status
Findings
        High no-show rate: About 20% of patients did not come to their appointments.
        More female patients: Around 65% of the patients are female, which is much higher than male patients.
        Younger patients miss more appointments: Patients who miss appointments are younger than those who attend.
        Hypertension is common: About 1 in 5 patients have hypertension, making it the most common condition.
        Appointments are consistently high: There are many appointments every day, but missed visits reduce efficiency.
Recommendations						
        Send reminders: Use SMS or phone calls to remind patients about appointments.
        Flexible scheduling: Offer evening or weekend appointments for busy patients.
        Educate patients about regular check-ups, especially for hypertension and diabetes.
